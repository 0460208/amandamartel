About Me

Hello! I'm Amanda Martel, a passionate full-stack web developer based in Springfield, Ohio. With a solid educational background, I hold an Associate of Applied Business in New Media with a focus on Web Design, and a Bachelor of Applied Science in Web Design and Development, specializing in Web Design.

My journey into the world of web development began with a curiosity for crafting visually stunning and intuitive user interfaces. Over time, I've come to cherish the harmonious blend of creativity and logic that defines my role as a full-stack developer.

My favorite programming languages, JavaScript and Python, serve as my trusty tools in bringing ideas to life. Whether it's orchestrating captivating front-end designs or architecting robust back-end systems, I find joy in the dynamic nature of my work.

What truly excites me about my career is the opportunity to explore the creative realm of front-end design, while also delving into the intricate logic of back-end programming. It's this balance of artistry and problem-solving that drives my passion and fuels my continuous growth as a developer.

In my spare time, you can find me experimenting with new technologies, refining my coding skills, or simply enjoying a good cup of coffee while sketching out new design concepts. I'm always eager to collaborate on exciting projects and contribute to the ever-evolving landscape of web development.

Thank you for visiting my online portfolio. Feel free to explore my work and don't hesitate to reach out if you'd like to discuss potential collaborations or just geek out about all things web development!
