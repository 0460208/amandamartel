About

My foundation in web design and development was laid at Clark State College, where I earned both an Associate of Applied Business in New Media with a focus on Web Design and a Bachelor of Applied Science in Web Design and Development, specializing in Web Design. These degrees have equipped me with a comprehensive understanding of the digital landscape and the skills to navigate it with finesse.

Before transitioning into the world of web development, I spent over a decade refining my customer service and teamwork skills in the food service industry. This experience taught me the importance of communication, adaptability, and collaboration – qualities that I carry into every project.

During my two years at Vivial as a web designer, I had the opportunity to refine my design skills and contribute to impactful digital solutions. Currently, I'm excelling as a social content creator at Thryv, where I've been for the nearly three years, continuously exploring new ways to engage audiences and drive results.

My aspiration is to join a full-stack web development team where I can leverage my expertise to turn ideas into reality. I thrive in collaborative environments and am eager to contribute to projects that push boundaries and inspire innovation.

Off the clock, you'll find me with my two cats - Leo and Dallas and one dog - Chubbs, indulging in art, especially painting and drawing, finding exciting new recipies to cook, and embarking on adventures in video games like Hogwarts Legacy.

So, that's me in a nutshell – a web developer with a love for all things digital and a passion for bringing creativity to life. Let's create something awesome together!
